Ariel Bio:

Ariel was one of 7 sisters. She lived under the sea in the city of Atlantica from a young age. Her father, King Triton ruled the underwater kingdom. All of her 6 royal sisters were content with their lives, but Ariel wanted more. Her fascination with the human world led her on dangerous and exciting treasure hunts. Her best friend, Flounder, often accompanied her. This also led to her befriending a noisy seagull named Scuttle. Ariel dreamed of becoming a human after meeting Prince Eric, and instantly fell in love with him. Her father disapproved, and destroyed the treasures she found in shipwrecks. Soon after, she bargained with the sea-witch Ursula, who promised to make her human for 3 days if Ariel gave Ursula her voice. If Ariel failed to make the Prince Eric fall in love with her and kiss her before the three days were up, she would have to return to the sea and belong to Ursula. If she did manage however, she could stay on land forever. During this very eventful time, Ariel almost succeeded in kissing Eric, something Ursula didn't want. Ursula transforms into a human named Vanessa, and puts a spell on Eric that makes him fall in love with a disguised Ursula. They are to be married on Eric's ship. With the help of her friends, Ariel makes it to the ship, where Scuttle breaks Vanessa's necklace. This breaks the spell and turns Ursula back into a sea-witch. Unfortunately, Ariel's voice returns too late, and she turns into her mermaid form. King Triton trades his life for that of his daughter's, Ursula gaining control of the seven seas. With her newfound powers, Ursula grows into her giant self and tries to kill Ariel, but Eric dives the main mast of a sunken ship in Ursula's body, killing her. In the end, King Triton sees how happy his daughter is and he uses his magical trident to turn Ariel into a human, and she and Eric get married.
