Meet the Counsellors


Daffy Duck

Daffy is no stranger to a strains in friendships. No matter what your friends may have done to offend you, Daffy's experienced way worse. No wonder he makes such a great counsellor! You can count on this duck to cheer you up whenever your other friends are acting incredibly DETHSPICABLE!


Kermit the Frog

You won't need to explain to Kermit what a challenge it is to keep that special someone happy. He's been on a rollercoaster of an on-again-off-again relationship with his girlfriend for YEARS! When it comes to romantic relationships, talk to the frog, he's got a lot of wisdom up those green sleeves!


Princess Ariel

With 6 sisters, you can bet Princess Ariel has learned a lot about how sisters operate. So even if you are totally lost on why your sister does what she does, Ariel should have no trouble clearing that up!
***Warning*** In-person meetings with Ariel will require you to be a certified scuba diver or a mermaid/merman. Otherwise, members are advised to contact her on her SHELL-phone or through E-mail.


Simon Seville from The Chipmunks

Simon has two brothers of his own that are definitely on opposite spectrums of the scale. On one hand, he's got sweet, innocent Theodore and on the other hand, he must deal with wild and rambunctious Alvin. Just tell him your bro stories and he should be able to relate very well!
