Healing Hearts, Mending Lives

What is Heart Helpers Counselling Services?

We all know that there are times when the people that we hold dearest and nearest to our hearts start acting KA-RAY-ZAY! Not picking up your calls, not answering your texts, not liking your new pictures on social media, avoiding meeting up with you, and so on. Or maybe you are the one being distant for one reason or another...

Now, you may be thinking, "What's up with THEM?" We all know that at those times, it's very important to take time out to try to work it out, but what if the outcome is less than favourable? Maybe all of this unusual behaviour was just a prelude to the revelation of a bigger problem in your relationship!
This is where we come in.

If you need advice, a shoulder to cry on, someone to help you take your mind off of it or just listening ears, here we are to help. Heart Helpers has employed several registered counsellors that are more than qualified to help you navigate any rocky relationship. However, our main goal is for this to be a temporary service for you while you repair or move on from your situation with your head held high!


Think of us as your "back-up best-friend" and we'll do our very best to make sure that your heart heals and you move on, happier and healthier than ever before!
