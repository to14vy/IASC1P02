Welcome to Heart Helper Counselling Services

Has your best friend refused to take your calls? Does your boyfriend seem to always be "working" whenever you want to hang out? Has your little sister grown out of wanting to chill with you? If so, you are in the right place!
Heart Helper Counselling Services is a service designed to help people like you get through challenging times in all types of relationships. Because, let's face it: not everyone like us all of the time. Sometimes, people just act strange towards us and we have no clue why! However, thanks to this wonderful service, you can connect with every-day individuals (who also double as qualified counsellors) who have gone through it before.
It's always nice to take to someone who gets it when your going THROUGH it and now, with just a click of a button, you can!

Well, what are you waiting for? Fill out our Match Up Form and get paired up with one of our counsellors today!
